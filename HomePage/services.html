Our Services

SHAMPOO

At Kombo Hair Salon, our shampoo services are more than just a rinse — they’re a rejuvenating experience. We use high-quality, salon-grade shampoos tailored to your hair type to cleanse, hydrate, and restore your hair’s natural shine. Whether you're preparing for a cut, color, or simply want to unwind, our shampoo service includes a relaxing scalp massage designed to promote circulation and reduce stress. From deep-cleansing treatments to moisture-rich formulas, we ensure your hair feels refreshed, healthy, and luxuriously soft after every wash.

HAIR TREATMENT

Our hair treatment services are designed to restore your hair’s health, strength, and shine. We offer a range of nourishing treatments tailored to your specific needs, including professional amino and keratin treatments that help smooth frizz, strengthen hair strands, and improve manageability. Whether your hair is dry, damaged, or chemically treated, our expert stylists use high-quality products to deeply hydrate and repair from the inside out—leaving your hair noticeably softer, shinier, and easier to style.

WOMEN HAIRCUT

We offer a variety of haircut styles tailored specifically for women. Whether you're looking for a bold transformation, a classic trim, or something completely unique, our skilled stylists are here to help you find the perfect cut to suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style. From precision bobs and soft layers to textured shags and trendy pixies, we take the time to understand your vision and deliver a look that makes you feel confident and refreshed.
